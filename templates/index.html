Frequently Asked Questions

Waybill delivery allows you to send packages directly from the pick-up point to your address. Our riders ensure timely delivery, keeping you updated every step of the way.

Handoff delivery ensures that your packages are delivered to the right person at the right time. Our riders are trained to verify the recipient's identity before handing over the package.

We pick up the food our customers order from their favorite restaurants, following detailed instructions for the pickup location, and deliver it to them. Our riders ensure the food arrives hot and fresh, right at your doorstep.

To create an account, simply click on the "Get Started" button on our homepage. Fill in the required details, and you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

To update your profile, log in to your account and navigate to the "Settings" section. From there, you can edit your personal details, such as your name, email, phone number, password, and address. Once you've made the changes, click the "Submit" button to update your profile information.

You can track your delivery through our app by logging into your account. Once logged in, navigate to the "Track Delivery" section, where you will find real-time updates on the location and status of your package.
